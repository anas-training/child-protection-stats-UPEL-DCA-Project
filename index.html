📚 Information Section

🏛️ What are Protection Court Files?

Protection court files are legal documents created when children need special care and protection from the court system. These files are opened when there are concerns about a child's safety, welfare, or wellbeing.

📊 Understanding the 75% Statistic

The data shows that 3 out of every 4 children (75%) in open protection cases are girls. This significant difference highlights the need for targeted support services and interventions that address the specific vulnerabilities and needs of girls in the child protection system.

🎯 Why This Matters

  • Resource Allocation: Helps organizations plan appropriate services and support programs
  • Awareness: Raises understanding about gender disparities in child protection needs
  • Prevention: Guides development of targeted prevention strategies
  • Training: Informs professionals about the populations they serve
